1. VOS Account Composition
VOS account is composed of:
u[s|pn|w|i]_xxxxxxxxx
 --source--
 

xxxxxxxx is number.

2. VOS Account Prefix Table
Prefix				Meaning
---------------------------
u					uutalk
s					from standard client
p					from OEM pre-installed
n					OEM number
w					from web
i					from invitation
